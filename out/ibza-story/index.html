The @iBzA Story

Every brand has an origin. This is mine — Laraib Siddiqui, the operator behind @iBzA.

CH-01

The Origin

It started in Aurangabad, Bihar — a small town, a big screen, and a curiosity for how things on the internet are built. No shortcuts, just self-driven learning and a refusal to stay average.

CH-02

The Craft

Years of writing code, breaking things, and rebuilding them better. From first lines of HTML to shipping full-stack web apps with React, Next.js and Node — the craft became an obsession.

CH-03

The Brand

Code alone wasn't enough. @iBzA was born — a brand that fuses engineering with identity and growth. Innovate · Build · Zeal · Awareness.

CH-04

The Structure

Now in Noida, the mission is to build a real structure: a multi-product ecosystem and a studio that helps others launch, grow, and make their own mark.
